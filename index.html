The Heart of the Shop

Impress is a cozy, small local shop proudly run by a dedicated duo: a 75+ year-old father and his 35+ year-old son.

Despite having a hearing disability, the son actively manages and operates the entire shop's technical and printing setup with absolute precision and care. When you support Impress, you are supporting a legacy of resilience, local family values, and unmatched community service in Shillong.
